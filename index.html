Projects

High School Project — Rube Goldberg Machine

Learned how to cooperate with group members.
Produced a robot car that could transfer a marble using Arduino Uno platform.
Our objective was to program the car so that it would wait for ten seconds for the ball to fall into a cup on it, after that, it then move along a predesigned route to the destination.
To let the car move along a certain track, we installed a infrared radiation sensor on our Arduino motherboard. This sensor can constantly send IR to the floor and detect reflection.
We tape the track using electrical tape, which can hardly reflect IR. Therefore, our can detect whether the car is on truck by monitoring the output of IR sensor.
Due to a lack of servo motor, we chose to send PWM signal to the motor of our car to control its direction.
